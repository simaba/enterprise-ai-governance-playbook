# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this repository.

## Scope

This repository is a practical playbook for enterprise AI governance and operating models. Helpful contributions improve clarity, structure, and operational usefulness.

## Good contributions

Examples of helpful contributions include:
- improving playbook clarity,
- strengthening templates,
- refining process guidance,
- adding realistic examples,
- correcting inaccuracies.

## Contribution expectations

Please keep contributions:
- concise,
- implementation-aware,
- useful in real organizational settings,
- free of proprietary or confidential material.

## Pull requests

When opening a pull request, explain:
- what changed,
- why it improves the repository,
- and whether it affects the playbook, templates, or supporting guidance.
